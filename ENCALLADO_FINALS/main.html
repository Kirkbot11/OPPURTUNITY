Your Guide to College Entrance Exams:
Get Ready for the Academic Year!

As the new academic year approaches, it’s time for students to gear up for one of the most important milestones: college entrance exams. Whether you’re a high school graduate or looking to transfer, understanding the exam schedules and requirements can set you on the right path to higher education.

Stay Informed About Exam Schedules

Each year, colleges and universities across the Philippines announce their entrance exam dates. It’s essential to keep an eye on these announcements so you can plan your preparation accordingly. Visit our website regularly for the latest updates on exam schedules from various institutions!

Tips for Success

Plan Ahead

Don’t hesitate to reach out for help from teachers, mentors, or online resources.

Stay Organized

Keep track of all important dates and document requirements to avoid last-minute rushes.

Seek Support

Start your preparations early. Review relevant subjects and practice with past exam papers to build your confidence.
